5-Min DIY recipes

stuffed monaco

Ingredients: monaco, tomato, grated-panner, capsicum, corainder, tomato-ketchup, green-chutney, cheese, chaat-masala, lemon-drops.
Directions: make a stuffing by mixing all the ingredients. stuff it between the monaco. garnish cheese and heat till the cheese melts. serve them fresh.

cheese chilli corn

Ingredients: 1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese, 2 tablespoons butter,4 cups fresh or frozen corn, 1 can (4 ounces) chopped green chilies,1/4 cup milk, 1/4 teaspoon garlic, 1/8 teaspoon salt, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
Directions: In a large saucepan, combine cream cheese and butter. Cook and stir over medium heat for 4-5 minutes or until smooth. Stir in the remaining ingredients. Cook for 5 minutes or until heated through. Serve with a slotted spoon.

Mugcake

Ingredients:4 tablespoons flour, 4 tablespoons sugar, 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa, 3 tablespoons milk, 3 tablespoons oil, 3 tablespoons chocolate chips, splash vanilla or other flavoring Directions: Add all of the dry ingredients to the mug and mix. Stir in milk and oil. Add chocolate chips* and splash of vanilla. Stir well.

Mac N Cheese

Ingredients:½ cup elbow macaroni, ½ cup water, 3 tablespoons milk, salt, pepper, ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese Directions:Mix the macaroni, water, and salt in a microwaveable mug. Microwave for 2-3 minutes, then stir. Add the milk, cheese, salt, and pepper, then stir. Microwave for another 30 seconds, stir, and garnish with a sprinkle of chives.Voila!

Popcorn balls

Ingredients:popcorn, marshmallows, butter.
Directions: Pop your popcorn. Melt your butter in a large bowl. Add marshmallows, and microwave for two minutes, stirring after one. Fold popcorn into mixture and form into balls. Enjoy.
