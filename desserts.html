Desserts

Chocolate Pudding 

INGREDIENTS

2/3 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweeteend cocoa powder like Hersheys Cocoa
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 tablespoons cornstarch
2 1/4 cups milk
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 tablespoon butter This just adds a bit of richness and a glossy look. optional

            

            

   METHOD  

  1. In medium saucepan combine sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt with a whisk.
  2. Add milk and continue to whisk. Heat to simmer over medium heat.
  3. Cook for 1-2 minutes until thick and bubbly.
  4. Remove from heat - add vanilla and butter
  5. Pour into your favorite dessert glass and chill throughly.

   Microwave Instructions.

   Stir together sugar, cocoa, cornstarch and salt in large microwave-safe bowl; gradually stir in milk.
   Microwave at HIGH (100%) 7 to 10 minutes or until mixture comes to full boil, stirring every few minutes.
   Stir in butter and vanilla. Pour into dishes to cool.

Mocha pots

    INGREDIENTS 

200g milk or dark chocolate with coffee, broken into chunks
300ml pot double cream
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 tbsp crème fraîche


            

     METHOD

  1.Melt the chocolate in the microwave for 2 mins, stirring halfway through, or over a pan of gently simmering water. Leave to
   cool a little.
  2.Using an electric whisk, whip the double cream with the vanilla in a bowl until lightly whipped. Fold in the cooled, melted
    chocolate until fully combined.
  3.Split the mixture between four small bowls or ramekins and serve topped with a dollop of crème fraîche. If you aren’t 
     serving straight away, chill in the fridge and then add the crème fraîche just before bringing to the table.
